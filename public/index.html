Vote from anywhere. Verify everything.

A remote, auditable, accessible voting system for Indian elections — engineered for slow phones, weak networks and small towns.

Why this exists

Built for the third of India that the polling booth misses.

Migrants, elders, persons with disabilities

Eligible voters who cannot reach their home polling station get a credentialled remote channel.

Cryptographic receipts

Every ballot returns a Merkle root receipt. Anyone can verify it against the public root.

WCAG 2.2 AAA, bilingual

English and हिन्दी, screen-reader-first, keyboard-first, reduced-motion respected.

Cloud Run, scale-to-zero

Single container, asia-east1, sub-second cold start, idle cost zero.
